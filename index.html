Green Ball 3D

Green Ball 3D is a series of 3d platformer ball-roller games made in Unity Engine. The first game was released on itch.io in 2017 as my first ever experience with software development. In the game you play as a green ball trying to get through simple puzzles and obstacles to the finish while collecting score points. A year later in 2018 I released Green Ball 2 - an imprved successor with more levels, new features and improved visuals The game is available for free to download at itch.io
